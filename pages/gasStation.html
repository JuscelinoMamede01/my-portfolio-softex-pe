Gas station DB Project

The objective is to track sales, collect valuable consumer data, analyze sales trends, predict future sales, identify business opportunities, and make informed pricing and inventory decisions.

Project Overview

The goal is to record sales and gather valuable information about the consumption of its products and services, analyze sales trends, predict future sales, identify business opportunities, and make informed decisions about pricing and inventory. By analyzing the recorded data, the gas station can identify which products are most popular among its customers and adjust its offerings accordingly, improving its products and services to meet customer needs and preferences. Consequently, this can lead to increased revenue and profitability.
